# Indian Institute of Technology Ropar
## Capacity Building Program on AI
### FDP For Faculty PAN India HEI

**A Ministry of Education, Government of India Initiative for Faculty Members of the Higher Education Institute**

Under the aegis of Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme (MMTTP)

---

## Program Overview

The role of artificial intelligence (AI) in education, particularly in enhancing the capabilities of faculty and Academic leaders, is very critical. Initiatives aimed at integrating AI into teacher training programs, developing AI-driven educational tools, and promoting research in AI to improve teaching methodologies will equip the Higher Education ecosystem to enhance overall quality.

### Primary Objectives

The primary objective of this initiative is to align AI training programs with the vision and directives of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, ensuring that faculty and academic leaders are equipped to foster holistic, multidisciplinary, and technology-integrated education. The program aims to:

- **Empower Educators**: Equip faculty with advanced knowledge and practical skills in Artificial Intelligence
- **Foster Innovation**: Enable adoption of innovative teaching methodologies and enhanced academic practices
- **Standardize Curriculum**: Establish standardized AI curriculum across higher education institutions
- **Promote Research**: Cultivate strong research and innovation culture among faculty
- **Enable Lifelong Learning**: Ensure educators remain abreast of evolving AI trends and pedagogical technologies
- **Ensure Inclusivity**: Extend training opportunities to diverse educators and uphold equity in education
- **Prepare for Future Workforce**: Prepare both teachers and students for meaningful participation in an AI-driven global workforce

## Evolution of the Program

### Phase 1: Traditional Zoom Sessions (Cohorts 1-2)
The program began with live Zoom sessions where participants interacted with experts and engaged in polls, assignments, and projects. Certification criteria were rigorous: 80% attendance, 80% poll attempts with 50% accuracy, completion of all assignments, and submission of final project.

### Phase 2: ViBe Integration (Cohorts 3-4)
The VLED Lab at IIT Ropar developed ViBe, an AI-enabled learning platform. A mix of Zoom sessions and ViBe content was introduced, providing greater flexibility for participants.

### Phase 3: ViBe-Centric Approach (Cohorts 5-7)
From Cohort 5 onwards, ViBe became the core of the FDP. Daily Zoom sessions continued for expert interaction and reflection, while ViBe provided asynchronous learning opportunities.

### Phase 4: Full Asynchronous Model (Cohort 8 onwards)
Complete transition to asynchronous learning on ViBe with project-based assessments, maintaining rigorous certification standards while providing maximum flexibility.

## Participant Feedback

### On the FDP
Faculty members described the FDP as one of the most valuable programs they had attended in recent years. Key highlights:

- Interactive sessions and relevant content
- Hands-on assignments and practical learning
- Exposure to new methodologies (engineered prompting, scripts, project-based learning)
- Increased confidence and professional development

**Common Suggestions:**
- More flexible timings (evenings, weekends, vacations)
- Longer deadlines for assignments

### On ViBe Platform
The ViBe platform received overwhelmingly positive feedback:

- **Flexibility**: Self-paced learning at participants' convenience
- **Engagement**: Short video segments and interactive quizzes kept learners attentive
- **Effectiveness**: Provided freedom to learn more effectively than long live sessions
- **Innovation**: Participants expressed interest in using ViBe in their own institutions

Many described ViBe as "addictive" and appreciated how it demanded full attention while providing instant feedback.

## Key Takeaways

The CBPAI journey demonstrates:

1. **Evolution Works**: Gradual transition from traditional to technology-enabled learning proved successful
2. **Flexibility Matters**: Asynchronous learning increased completion rates and participant satisfaction
3. **Faculty Appreciation**: Teachers valued the practical, hands-on approach to AI training
4. **Innovation Pays Off**: ViBe platform became integral to program success and participant engagement
5. **Inclusive Education**: Program successfully reached faculty across all of India, achieving near-perfect geographic coverage

---

## Conclusion

The Capacity Building Program on Artificial Intelligence under MMTTP has grown from a traditional FDP into a comprehensive, technology-enabled initiative that has reached over 1,000 faculty members across India. With the introduction of ViBe, the program has not only improved certification rates but also created a meaningful learning experience that faculty truly value.

This is just the beginning. With continued Ministry support and the trust of faculty nationwide, CBPAI and ViBe will continue to evolve, shaping the future of professional development in Indian higher education.
