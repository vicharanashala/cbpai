## Constructive Suggestions

While overall feedback was extremely positive, participants also shared thoughtful suggestions for improvement:

### Scheduling & Pacing Suggestions

**Most Requested Features:**
1. **Evening Sessions**: Classes after 6 PM to avoid teaching hours
2. **Weekend Options**: Saturday/Sunday sessions for convenience
3. **Holiday Scheduling**: During academic breaks and vacations
4. **Longer Deadlines**: More time for assignments balancing teaching load
5. **Batch Sessions**: Options for different time zones if possible

### Technical Improvements Noted

**Issues Reported (in ViBe):**
- Videos occasionally restarting abruptly
- Progress tracking showing incorrect status
- Repeated questions during content replay
- Interface learning curve for new users
- Minor bugs disrupting experience

**Participants' Perspective:**
- Generally acknowledged as "early stage platform issues"
- Viewed with understanding rather than frustration
- Expressed confidence in improvements
- Did not diminish overall appreciation

### Content & Delivery Enhancements

- More advanced modules for experienced educators
- Domain-specific AI applications (science, humanities, etc.)
- Deeper exploration of selected topics
- More international perspectives
- Industry partnerships and guest experts
