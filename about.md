## About the Program

The **Capacity Building Program on Artificial Intelligence (CBPAI)** is a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Education, Government of India, designed to equip faculty members across the nation with practical knowledge and skills in using AI for education.

### Program Details

**Implementing Institution**: Indian Institute of Technology Ropar
**Facilitating Lab**: VLED Lab (Virtual Learning Experience Design Lab)
**Framework**: Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme (MMTTP)
**Duration**: February - September 2025
**Total Cohorts**: 10

### Introduction

Between February and September 2025, IIT Ropar, through its VLED Lab, conducted ten cohorts with the participation of 1,055 faculty members from all 28 states and 8 union territories of India, along with one international participant from Dubai.

The program began with live Zoom sessions where participants interacted with experts and engaged in polls, assignments, and projects. From Cohort 3 onwards, the lab introduced **ViBe**, an AI-enabled learning platform that became the cornerstone of the program.

### What is ViBe?

ViBe is an innovative, AI-enabled learning platform developed by IIT Ropar's VLED Lab. It provides faculty with:

- **Flexibility**: Learn at your own pace through interactive videos
- **Engagement**: Segment-based activities and interactive quizzes
- **Structure**: Rigorous certification standards maintained throughout
- **Accessibility**: Content available 24/7 for asynchronous learning

### Program Approach: From Classrooms to AI-Driven Solutions

Our curriculum doesn't just teach AI concepts in isolation. Instead, we follow a **case study approach** that addresses real challenges faculty face:

- **Real-World Problems**: Start with everyday challenges teachers encounter in their classrooms
- **Practical Solutions**: Introduce AI-powered tools and techniques to solve these problems
- **Hands-On Learning**: Faculty automate tasks, build dashboards, create lesson plans with AI, and develop portfolios
- **Immediate Impact**: Participants leave with practical skills they can immediately apply in their institutions

### Gender Inclusivity

The program achieved excellent gender balance:
- **Female Participants**: 572 (54%)
- **Male Participants**: 483 (46%)

This demonstrates the program's success in reaching and engaging women faculty in AI education.

### The Journey to ViBe

**Cohorts 1-2**: Traditional Zoom-based delivery with strict certification criteria

**Cohorts 3-4**: Introduction of ViBe alongside Zoom sessions; faculty appreciated the flexibility

**Cohorts 5-7**: ViBe as primary platform with daily Zoom support sessions

**Cohorts 8+**: Full asynchronous model on ViBe with project-based assessments

### Results and Impact

The transition to ViBe has yielded impressive results:

- **Improved Certification Rates**: From 10-16% (Cohorts 3-4) to 24-43% (Cohorts 5-7+)
- **Participant Satisfaction**: Faculty described it as a "flexible learning companion"
- **Emotional Connection**: Many participants became "addicted" to the platform
- **Institutional Interest**: Faculty express desire to adopt ViBe in their own institutions

### Vision for the Future

CBPAI under MMTTP is more than just a training program—it represents:

- A commitment to AI-enabled education across Indian higher education
- A model for flexible, participant-centric professional development
- An example of technology serving pedagogical needs, not replacing them
- A collective journey toward an AI-aware educator community

With continued Ministry of Education support and the trust of faculty nationwide, we are confident this is just the beginning of a transformative initiative in Indian education.
