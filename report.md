# CBPAI Program Report

## Executive Summary

The Capacity Building Program on Artificial Intelligence (CBPAI) under the Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme (MMTTP) has successfully completed 10 cohorts between February and September 2025, training **1,055 faculty members** from across India and one international participant.

---

## Enrollment Data

A total of 1,055 participants registered across the ten cohorts. Out of these, **228 participants have successfully completed all requirements and received certificates**, representing approximately 22% certification rate as of October 17, 2025.

## Methods Used for Session Delivery

### Evolution of Delivery Methods

**Cohorts 1-2: Traditional Zoom-based (Feb-April 2025)**
- Live expert sessions
- Polls, assignments, and final projects
- Rigorous criteria: 80% attendance, 80% poll attempts with 50% accuracy
- Case study approach focused on real classroom problems

**Cohorts 3-4: Hybrid Approach (May-June 2025)**
- Introduction of ViBe platform alongside Zoom
- Mix of synchronous and asynchronous learning
- Faculty responded positively to flexibility

**Cohorts 5-7: ViBe-Centric (June-August 2025)**
- ViBe as core platform
- Daily Zoom sessions for discussion and reflection
- Certification rates improved significantly

**Cohort 8+: Full Asynchronous (September onwards)**
- Complete transition to ViBe modules
- Project-based assessment
- Multiple Entry, Multiple Exit mode
- Flexible completion timelines

### Pedagogical Approach: Case Study Method

Rather than teaching abstract AI concepts, the program uses real-world problems as entry points:

1. **Identify Real Challenges**: Classroom automation, student engagement tracking, lesson planning
2. **Introduce AI Solutions**: Practical tools like engineered prompting, Google Sheets automation, AI writing assistants
3. **Hands-On Practice**: Faculty build:
   - Automated Google Sheets using App Scripts
   - Dashboards for class engagement tracking
   - AI-powered lesson plans
   - GitHub portfolios and websites

4. **Immediate Application**: Skills participants can use in their institutions right away

## Certification Trends with ViBe

The adoption of ViBe has had a dramatic positive impact on certification:

**Pre-ViBe Period (Cohorts 1-4)**
- Average certification rate: 18%
- Only Cohort 1 exceeded 40%
- Cohorts 3-4 saw steep decline (12%-10%)

**ViBe Adoption Period (Cohorts 5-7)**
- Cohort 5: 24%
- Cohort 6: 28%
- Cohort 7: 43%

This improvement of 40%+ from the lowest point demonstrates the effectiveness of flexible, asynchronous learning platforms in improving completion rates.

## Feedback on FDP

### Participant Testimonials

Faculty members described the FDP as one of the most valuable programs they had attended in recent years:

> "Best FDP I have ever attended"

> "The journey from chalkboards to chatbots is not just about technology—it's about redefining our role as educators"

### Strengths Highlighted

- Interactive sessions with expert speakers
- Relevant, practical content
- Hands-on assignments and case studies
- Exposure to new methodologies (engineered prompting, scripts, AI tools)
- Direct enhancement of teaching confidence
- Professional development value

### Constructive Suggestions

Most common requests from participants:

1. **Flexible Timings**: Evening, weekend, and vacation sessions to accommodate teaching schedules
2. **Extended Deadlines**: Longer submission periods for assignments
3. **Balanced Scheduling**: Better consideration of academic calendar

### Overall Assessment

The FDP was perceived as well-organized, meaningful, and inspiring. Participants expressed strong gratitude and significant interest in attending similar or advanced programs in the future.

---

## Feedback on ViBe Platform

### Positive Responses

The ViBe platform received **overwhelming positive feedback**:

- **Flexibility**: "ViBe allowed me to learn at my own pace without compromising program depth"
- **Engagement**: "The short video segments kept me attentive and engaged"
- **Addictive Quality**: Many participants described becoming "addicted" to the platform
- **Professional Value**: "I want to use ViBe in my own institution"
- **Effective Learning**: Participants felt ViBe provided more effective learning than long live sessions

### Technical Challenges Noted

While overall reception was very positive, some technical issues were reported:

- Videos occasionally restarting abruptly
- Incorrect progress tracking
- Repeated questions during replays
- Interface learning curve for some users
- Minor bugs affecting user experience

### User Sentiment

Despite technical challenges, participants expressed genuine affection for ViBe:

- Appreciated how it demanded full attention
- Valued instant feedback mechanisms
- Expressed enthusiasm about its potential as a standard learning tool
- Many expressed desire to see ViBe adopted more widely in higher education

### Summary

ViBe has won the trust of participants. They see it as more than a platform—as a supportive learning space that respects their time, adapts to their pace, and makes professional development more personal and rewarding.

---

## Conclusion

The journey of CBPAI under MMTTP represents a successful evolution from traditional faculty development to technology-enabled, participant-centric professional training. Key takeaways:

1. **Scale & Reach**: Successfully trained 1,055 faculty across all of India
2. **Inclusivity**: Achieved near-perfect gender balance (54% female, 46% male)
3. **Geographic Breadth**: Reached all 28 states and 8 union territories
4. **Flexible Learning Works**: ViBe adoption directly improved certification rates
5. **Faculty Engagement**: Strong emotional connection to the program and platform
6. **Practical Impact**: Faculty report immediate application of learning
7. **Continuous Improvement**: Feedback is valued and drives ongoing refinement

With Ministry of Education support and continued faculty trust, CBPAI represents just the beginning of transforming AI literacy in Indian higher education. ViBe will continue to evolve as a model platform for flexible, effective professional development.
