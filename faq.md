# Frequently Asked Questions

## About CBPAI

### What is CBPAI?

CBPAI (Capacity Building Program on Artificial Intelligence) is a flagship initiative under the Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme (MMTTP) by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. It's designed to equip faculty members across India with practical knowledge and skills in using AI for education.

### Who can participate?

The program is open to faculty members from Higher Education Institutions across India. This includes professors, associate professors, assistant professors, and other academic staff.

### What are the prerequisites?

No prior AI experience is required. The program is designed for educators of all technical backgrounds. Basic familiarity with computers and internet is helpful.

### How much does it cost?

The program is free for all participants. It's funded by the Ministry of Education, Government of India.

---

## Program Details

### What will I learn?

The program covers:
- AI fundamentals and applications in education
- Practical AI tools (ChatGPT, Google Sheets automation, etc.)
- Engineered prompting techniques
- Building dashboards for student engagement
- Lesson planning with AI
- Creating digital portfolios and websites
- Project-based learning methodologies

### How long is the program?

The program typically runs for 4-8 weeks depending on the cohort and delivery mode. You can progress at your own pace, especially in ViBe-based cohorts.

### Is there a schedule I must follow?

It depends on the cohort:
- **Traditional Cohorts**: Fixed schedule with live Zoom sessions
- **ViBe-based Cohorts**: Flexible, self-paced learning. Study anytime, anywhere.

### Can I get a certificate?

Yes! Upon successful completion of all program requirements, including assignments and final project, you receive a certificate from IIT Ropar under the MMTTP.

### What are the certification requirements?

Requirements vary by cohort type:
- **Traditional**: 80% attendance, 80% poll attempts with 50% accuracy, all assignments, final project
- **ViBe-based**: Complete all ViBe modules and submit the capstone project

## ViBe Platform

### What is ViBe?

ViBe is an AI-enabled learning platform developed by IIT Ropar's VLED Lab. It provides self-paced, interactive learning through short video segments, quizzes, and projects.

### Is ViBe difficult to use?

No, ViBe is designed to be user-friendly. It has an intuitive interface and most users become comfortable within a few sessions. Technical support is available if needed.

### Can I access ViBe on my phone?

Yes! ViBe is responsive and works on desktops, tablets, and smartphones. You can access your learning anytime, anywhere.

### What if I have internet issues?

While ViBe requires internet connection, some content can be accessed offline once loaded. If you experience persistent connectivity issues, contact the support team.

### How much time do I need to spend on ViBe weekly?

This is flexible! ViBe segments are 5-15 minutes each. You could spend 30 minutes per day or batch them over weekends. Most participants find 3-5 hours per week manageable alongside teaching.

### Can I go back and review content?

Absolutely! You can review any content anytime. ViBe tracks your progress, so you can pick up where you left off.

## Learning Experience

### Will this help me teach better?

Yes! The program focuses on practical, classroom-applicable skills. Many participants report implementing AI tools in their teaching immediately after the program.

### Can I apply what I learn in my own institution?

Definitely! That's the entire goal. The program is designed with real classroom problems in mind, and participants leave with practical, implementable skills.

### Is there peer interaction?

Yes! In ViBe-based cohorts, there are discussion forums where you can interact with peers from across India. Many lasting professional connections are formed.

### Will there be live support from instructors?

In most cohorts, yes. Even in primarily asynchronous programs, instructors are available through forums, email, and occasional synchronous sessions.

### What if I fall behind?

No problem! ViBe's flexible model means you can catch up anytime. The support team is also available to help if you're struggling with content.

## Technical & Administrative

### How do I enroll?

Check our main website for current cohort openings. Registration details and application information will be provided.

### What if I can't complete by the deadline?

Depending on the cohort model, there may be flexibility. Cohorts using Multiple Entry, Multiple Exit (MeMe) mode allow more flexibility. Contact the program coordinators for your specific situation.

### What if I need to drop out?

You can communicate with the program coordinators. Depending on when you withdraw, you may be able to join a future cohort or receive a certificate of participation.

### Will my institution know about my participation?

If you provide your institutional email and consent, your institution may receive notification of your participation and certification. This is optional.

### Who do I contact with technical issues?

Technical support is available through the ViBe platform and email. Response times are typically within 24 hours.

### What if I have questions about my certificate?

Contact the CBPAI program coordinators. They can verify your completion status and address any certificate-related questions.
