## Capacity Building Program on Artificial Intelligence Marks Historic Milestone

The **Capacity Building Program on Artificial Intelligence (CBPAI)** under the Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme (MMTTP) has successfully completed ten cohorts, reaching **1,055 faculty members** across all 28 states and 8 union territories of India.

### Key Achievements

**Widespread Geographic Impact**
- All 28 States covered
- 8 Union Territories represented
- 1 international participant from Dubai

**Gender-Inclusive Participation**
- Female Participants: 572 (54%)
- Male Participants: 483 (46%)

**Certification Growth with ViBe**
- Cohorts 1-4 (Pre-ViBe): Average 18% certification
- Cohort 7 (Full ViBe): 43% certification
- Overall: 228 certified out of 1,055 registered (22%)

### Top Contributing States

1. Tamil Nadu - 164 participants
2. Karnataka - 149 participants
3. Telangana - 130 participants
4. Punjab - 99 participants
5. Maharashtra - 87 participants

### The ViBe Platform Difference

The introduction of ViBe transformed the program with:
- Self-paced, flexible learning
- Interactive video segments and quizzes
- 24/7 accessibility
- Significantly improved certification rates

Faculty describe ViBe as an innovative platform that respects their time and enables meaningful professional development.

### Faculty Feedback Highlights

> "Best FDP I have ever attended"

> "The journey from chalkboards to chatbots is about redefining our role as educators"

Faculty appreciated the practical, hands-on approach and the flexibility to balance learning with their teaching schedules.

### What's Next

The success of CBPAI demonstrates strong demand for:
- AI literacy among educators
- Flexible, technology-enabled professional development
- Inclusive training reaching all regions of India
- Practical skills applicable in classrooms immediately

The program continues to evolve, with ViBe at its heart, preparing India's educators for an AI-driven future.
